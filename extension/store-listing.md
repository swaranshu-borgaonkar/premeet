## Privacy Practices

### Single Purpose
PrepMeet provides meeting context and note management for professionals by integrating with their calendar to deliver pre-appointment summaries and post-meeting note capture.

### Permission Justifications

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|---|---|
| identity | Connects your Google account for secure calendar access |
| alarms | Schedules 5-minute pre-meeting notifications |
| notifications | Alerts you before meetings and reminds you to take notes |
| storage | Saves your settings and caches notes for offline access |
| offscreen | Enables voice-to-text transcription (audio processed locally only) |

### Host Permission Justifications

| Host | Why We Need It |
|---|---|
| googleapis.com | Fetches calendar events and sends email summaries via Gmail |
| *.supabase.co | Syncs your encrypted notes and contacts across devices |

### Data Usage Promise
- **We collect:** Calendar event metadata (titles, times, attendee emails), notes you create, contact information from calendar events
- **We never collect:** Calendar event content/descriptions, email content, browsing history, passwords
- **We never sell:** Your data to anyone, ever
- **You control:** Export all your data anytime, delete your account permanently
- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
